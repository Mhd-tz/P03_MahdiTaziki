My Story

As a student at Simon Fraser University studying Interactive Arts & Technology, I bridge the gap between creative aesthetic systems and robust computational logic.

I specialize in building full-stack web applications prioritizing clean code, comprehensive CSS architectures, and accessible user experiences.

My Values

  • Content First: Design should frame, not overpower, the information.
  • Performance: Fast, accessible DOM structures over heavy libraries.
  • Precision: High attention to fluid scaling and cross-device testing.
